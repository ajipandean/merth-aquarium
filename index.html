About Merth Aquarium

Merth Aquarium is national public aquarium located within Ocean National Park in Bali. Merth Aquarium is home to thousands of species of fish across the world. Merth Aquarium has surpassed 1 trillion visitors on April 2019 and nominated to be the most sucessfull public aquarium in the world.
